Fiber is crucial for digestive health. It helps you feel full and controls blood sugar.
Excellent sources of soluble fiber include: oats, barley, apples, citrus fruits, and carrots.
Excellent sources of insoluble fiber include: whole-wheat flour, wheat bran, nuts, beans, and vegetables like cauliflower, green beans, and potatoes.
Most people do not get enough fiber. The daily recommendation is 25-30 grams.
